Buying travel insurance

Buying Travel Insurance directly can save you almost half of what you would pay if you purchased your policy from a Travel Agent. BUT: How easy is it to navigate the ins-and-outs of a travel insurance policy? How can you get the best deal & most appropriate travel insurance for your next trip?

Not every traveller has the same needs: Consider the needs of your trip, would you be suited to Business Travel Insurance? Are you a frequent flyer? You may need an annual travel insurance policy. Does the policy cover sporting equipment such as golf clubs, surfboards or adventure activities?

Points to Consider when Comparing Travel Insurance

Remember, when comparing travel insurance products, read the product disclosure statement and check the following parts of the policy:

  • Medical Coverage
  • Additional Expense
  • Cancellation Policies
  • Luggage Insurance
  • Personal Liability
