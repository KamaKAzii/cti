Policy benefits
(View policy wording)

+
Cancellation Costs *
  1. $10,000
Loss of deposits and fees paid for transport and accommodation due to unforeseen cancellation. Includes course fees, travel agency cancellation fees and loss of frequent flyer or equivalent points.
+
Additional Expenses / Medical Evacuation
  1. $10,000
Covers additional expense incurred for travel and accommodation if your trip is interrupted from a listed event which is outside you control. Includes return home following the sudden illness or serious injury of a close relative in Australia and, when agreed by us, medical evacuation home or to the nearest appropriate medical facility.
+
Accidental Death
  1. $10,000
A benefit payable to your estate in the event of your death within 12 months as a direct result of an injury sustained during your trip.
+
Luggage and Personal Effects *
  1. $4,000
Cover for your Luggage and Personal Effects following theft, accidental loss or accidental damage. Includes expenses for emergency essentials if your luggage is delayed.
+
Personal Liability
  1. $1,000,000
Cover for you being legally liable for causing bodily injury to others or causing damage to their property, including legal defence costs.
+
Rental Vehicle Insurance Excess Reimbursement
  1. $4,000
Cover for the rental vehicle insurance excess you are required to pay following an accident, damage or theft of your rental vehicle.
Rental Vehicle Return
  1. $500
Costs to return a rental vehicle to the rental agency if you are medically unfit to do so.
+
Snow Skiing Piste Closure *
  1. $500
An allowance for each day the skiing facilities at the resort you pre-booked before your trip and where you’re staying are totally closed due to adverse snow conditions.
+
Snow Skiing / Golf Pre-paid Costs *
  1. $200
Cover for loss of prepaid lift passes, green fees, equipment hire and tuition fees if you cannot ski/golf for more than 24 hours due to sudden illness or serious injury.
+
Snow Skiing / Golf Equipment Replacement *
  1. $200
Cover for hiring replacement equipment if yours is lost, damaged, or stolen or it is delayed or misdirected for more than 24 hours.
